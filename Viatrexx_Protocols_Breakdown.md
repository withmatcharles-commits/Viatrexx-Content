# **Viatrexx Therapeutic Protocols Detailed Breakdown**

This document outlines 11 specific therapeutic protocols designed by Viatrexx. Each section details the focus, standard administration, and specific product combinations for various conditions.

## **1\. Biological & Parasitic Protocol**

**Focus:** Addressing challenges from Candida, parasites, bacteria, and other biological factors that impair the body's ability to absorb nutrition.

### **Standard Protocol Instructions**

* **Dosage:** 4 squirts, 3 times a day.  
* **Administration:** Can be taken together, added to water, sprayed over a desired area, or used with a roller.  
* **Duration:** Typically 3 months. Adjustments or changes are usually made after 2 months.  
* **Note:** Irritability, cravings, moodiness, and withdrawal symptoms may occur during the cleansing process.

### **Common Core Protocol**

* **Months 1-2:**  
  * Viatrexx-P'sites  
  * Viatrexx-Candida+  
  * Viatrexx-Systemic Dx  
* **Month 3 (Maintenance/Transition):**  
  * Replace Viatrexx-Systemic with Viatrexx-GI.  
  * Evaluate if Viatrexx-Candida+ and/or Viatrexx-P'sites need to be continued.

### **Targeted Options**

* **Bacteria:** Viatrexx-B'teria  
* **Candida (Precursors, By-products & Terrain):** Viatrexx-Candida+ (Follow with Candida+ if starting with Candida1)  
* **Gentler Candida Cleanse:** Viatrexx-Candida1  
* **Fungal:** Viatrexx-F'gus  
* **System/Gut Support:** Viatrexx-GI, Viatrexx-Systemic, Viatrexx-Relief 3-G12 (Extra gut support)  
* **Amoebas:** Viatrexx-Am'bas  
* **Parasites:** Viatrexx-P'sites

**Add-on:** If energy gets low, consider Viatrexx-Intra Cell and a gender-specific Viatrexx product.
