* *Legacy:* viatrexx.com/products/adipose

### **2\. Meta Descriptions with "Trust Signals"**

Meta descriptions are your 160-character sales pitch. Every description should follow this formula: **\[Problem\] \+ \[Solution\] \+ \[The Viatrexx Difference\].**

* *Example:* "Struggling with systemic toxicity? Viatrexx Systemic Dx offers professional-grade matrix drainage using structured water and organic sourcing. Calibrated spray delivery for maximum absorption."

### **3\. Header Tag Hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)**

Search engines use headers to determine the "Weight" of a topic. Use this structure for product and blog pages:

* **H1:** \[Product Name\] \- \[Primary Condition Search Term\] (e.g., *Viatrexx MuSkel \- Natural Sports Injury Recovery*)  
* **H2:** The Science of Bio-Incorporated Regeneration (Introduces the "How")  
* **H3:** Key Benefits for \[Specific Symptom: e.g., Muscle Repair & Inflammation Control\]
